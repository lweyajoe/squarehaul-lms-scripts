The Garden of Eden: Biblical Perspective (Kindle Edition)

Step into the world’s first paradise, where life was perfect—until it wasn’t. "The Garden of Eden – A Biblical Perspective" takes you on a fascinating journey back to the origins of humanity, exploring the creation of man, the idyllic life in the Garden, and the pivotal moment that changed everything. God gave Adam and Eve one simple rule, but when temptation came in the form of a forbidden fruit, they couldn't resist taking a bite.


But what exactly was in that fruit that caused so much chaos? This book doesn’t just tell you what happened; it delves into the nature of the fruit itself, the ripple effects of that fateful decision, and the havoc it unleashed on the world. It's a thoughtful, and at times, cheeky analysis of how one moment in paradise continues to shape our lives today.


Whether you’re a religious leader, a Christian, a theologian, or just someone curious about what went wrong in the Garden, this book is for you. Come for the ancient story—stay for the fresh insights.
